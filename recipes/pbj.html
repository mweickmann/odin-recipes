IT'S PEANUT BUTTER JELLY TIME!

Description

Baseball bat not included.

Ingredients

  • The most amazing honey wheat bread
  • Your favorite peanut butter- creamy or crunchy
  • Jam, jelly, or preserves in the flavor of your choosing
  • (Optional) A tall glass of milk

Steps

  1. Place two pieces of bread on a plate.
  2. Cover both pieces of bread with a layer of peanut butter.
  3. Spread a generous amount of jam, jelly, or preserves over one slice
  4. Cover with the other slice of bread, slice in half diagonally.
  5. Pour a tall glass of cold milk and enjoy.
