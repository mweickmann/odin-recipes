Scrambled Eggs

Description

Somtimes the simplest breakfast is the best.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • (Optional) Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Melt one tbsp butter in skillet on medium heat.
  2. Crack eggs into bowl. Scramble.
  3. Add eggs to skillet. Salt generously.
  4. Mix frequently as egg cooks, breaking up larger pieces.
  5. Remove from heat just prior to being cooked and let residual heat finish the job.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and enjoy.
