CHEESEBURGER!

Description

Cheeseburgers are God's gift to America. Enjoy often.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Egg
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Turn on grill to preheat- as hot as possible.
  2. Form ground beef into 1/4lb patties.
  3. Season ground beef.
  4. Add to hot grill, cook 3.5 minutes per side.
  5. With 45 seconds remaining on final side, add cheese to each patty.
  6. Remove from heat and let rest. Add buns to grill. Toast for 45 seconds.
  7. Remove buns. Dress burger to taste.
  8. Eat that shit!
